# Hero Carousel Images

Place 6 high-quality images in this directory for the homepage hero carousel.

## Required Images:

1. **slide1.jpg** - Campus/School exterior view
   - Recommended: Wide shot of the school building
   - Size: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)

2. **slide2.jpg** - Students Learning/Classroom
   - Recommended: Students in a classroom setting
   - Size: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)

3. **slide3.jpg** - School Facilities
   - Recommended: Library, lab, or computer room
   - Size: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)

4. **slide4.jpg** - Sports & Activities
   - Recommended: Students playing sports or doing activities
   - Size: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)

5. **slide5.jpg** - Teachers/Faculty
   - Recommended: Teachers with students or in classroom
   - Size: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)

6. **slide6.jpg** - Graduation/Success
   - Recommended: Graduation ceremony or celebrating students
   - Size: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)

## Image Guidelines:

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** 1920x1080px minimum (Full HD)
- **File Size:** Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- **Quality:** High quality, well-lit, professional photos
- **Orientation:** Landscape (horizontal)
- **Subject:** Should represent the school positively
